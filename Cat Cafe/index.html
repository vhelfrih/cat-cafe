Our Cats

We house adopted cats while also being a neighborhood retreat that allows our community to come to relax and relieve stress by bonding with our friendly kitties.

Reservations

Our dear guests can choose from a wide range of hot and soft drinks, as well as cakes and hot sandwiches. We offer the opportunity to organize private events, birthdays and other occasions. We welcome everyone who loves kittens.

Contact

Our Cat Café awaits its dear guests in a quiet corner of downtown Tokyo. You can have a special experience in the company of saved kittens. Enjoy your favorite cookies and coffee with our purring and playful cats.
